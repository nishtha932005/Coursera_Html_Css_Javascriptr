Best Poems of Nishtha Kashyap

To My Life

Dearly I yearn for life's sweet embrace, Life's warm sunlight, my grace.
Life's gentle touch upon my face,
Moments of life, a cherished trace.
And I so embrace this life
For in its rhythm, I survive,
If I departed from this strife,
Life's essence would revive.
Place me, if I come anew,
As a breeze in your hair, let your laughter
Echo through the hills and dew,
Baptized by the joyous thereafter.
Bind us with a strand of time,
With moments strung from the fabric of days.
I could ascend to sublime heights,
Entrust my spirit to life's endless maze,
If I but touch the pulse of existence.

Place me, if ever I return,
In the rivers that flow, a current untamed,
On the mountains, a silhouette unframed.
Feeble without life's rhythmic rhyme,
I can no longer chime.

I am aged
Return to me the dreams of youth,
That I may navigate the path of truth,
Back with the drifting clouds,
Back to life's embrace, so proud.

- Inspired by Mahmoud Darwish
Mahmoud Darwish (1941-2008)
was an award-winning Palestinian author and poet.
His literature, particularly his poetry, created a sense of Palestinian identity and was used to resist the occupation of his homeland.

I Am Quiet

I am quiet and so is life.
Sipping moments with zest, while life sips strife.
That's the dance between us.
Like life, I wear colors broad and bright,
And like life, I read the script of the night.
Life doesn't see my silent gaze.
I don't see life's mysterious ways.
Life is quiet and so am I.
It poses questions to the universe.
I pose questions, a poetic converse…
A black cat strolls in the moonlight's gleam.
I feel the mystery of its fur,
And life feels the mystery of its stir…
I don't say to life: The world today
is clear and blue.
Life doesn't say to me: The world today is clear.
Life watches, and I watch back

and life is watched, and I am the watcher.
I move a step in life's grand plan.
Life moves a step in its eternal span.
I hum the melody of existence
and life hums the melody of its own subsistence.
I ponder: Is life the mirror reflecting me?
And turn to peer into its depths…but no reflections I see.
I leave the theatre of life.
I wonder: Maybe life's an actor…
or maybe a wanderer who thinks
I am a traveler.
Life is afraid…and so am I.

- Inspired by Mahmoud Darwish

I Come From Life

I come from life, a tapestry of memories
Born into moments, I have a journey
And a path with many turns,
I have challenges, joys,
And a window to the future's yearns.
Mine is the wave, dancing with the breeze,
I have my own perspective,
And an extra moment to seize.
Mine is the moon at the edge of dreams,
And the symphony of birds,
And the evergreen tree of life.
I tread this terrain before the storms
Turn its vibrant pulse into a dance floor.

I come from life. I offer the sky to the day
When the day sings its vibrant display.
And I shed tears to make myself known
To a passing cloud, not alone.
I learned all the words that echo in the heart
So that I could play my part.
I learned all the words and rearranged them
To create a single word: Existence.....

- Inspired by Nishtha Kashyap
